Meet Our Founder

Subodh Kumar Sinha

Founder

With over 15 years of experience in the financial services industry, Subodh Kumar Sinha founded Aapka Nivesh with a clear vision: to make financial planning simple, accessible, and effective for everyone.

His deep expertise in mutual funds and investment strategies has empowered thousands of investors—both individuals and institutions—to make informed financial decisions and achieve their long-term goals.

At Aapka Nivesh, Subodh offers personalized investment solutions based on each client's unique financial situation. Whether you're just starting out or managing complex portfolios, he ensures that every investor gets guidance tailored to their needs, goals, and risk appetite.

Beyond mutual funds, he also suggests other suitable investment options, ensuring a well-rounded approach to financial growth.

Through this platform, he is committed to educating investors, helping them understand their options, and making the investment journey transparent and confident.
